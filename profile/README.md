<div>&nbsp;</div>
<img align="right" width="100" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/aafd2164-cf63-4b9b-84dd-9a308d1b1b16" />

<p>OpenArchiCN 是一个专注于建筑全产业链数字化的开源组织。我们汇聚了一群拥有开源精神和跨界探索精神的理想主义者，致力于通过数字化技术打破专业藩篱，分享最前沿的建筑科技知识，构建开放的行业生态。我们的价值观是：数字驱动建筑，和建设者一起进化。</p>
<div>&nbsp;</div>
<p>OpenArchiCN is an open-source organization dedicated to the digitalization of the entire AEC (Architecture, Engineering, and Construction) industry chain. We bring together a group of idealists with an open-source ethos and a spirit of cross-disciplinary exploration, committed to breaking professional barriers, sharing cutting-edge AEC knowledge, and building an open industry ecosystem. Our core value is: Digital-Driven AEC, growing alongside builders.</p>
